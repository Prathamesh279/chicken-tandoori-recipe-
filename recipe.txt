//recipe;

first take ginger garlic paste 
then add it in yougurt and 
add red chilli powder and turmeric powder 
salt and mit it well with chicken pieces and 
fry it in frying pan 
then after frying take a piece of coal and 
put it in the glass and keep the chicken piece in bowl 
and keep coal glass in the bowl of chicken 
and put hot oil in the glass of coal - ("cover it for giving tandoori flavour")
and cover it with a plate or bowl 10 to 15 minutes
and our chicken tandoori is prepared have fun eatong it.
